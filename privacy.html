No sensitive information is saved

In order to build a utility for our data colleagues, we do not save any charts or sensitive information.

We only track the following:

  • Emails: Purely for user feedback and tracking... no spam!
  • Activity: We log anonymized activity like uploads, clicks and downloads. But we DON'T save the images of charts or the underlying data exported.
